# BOM Studios — Brand Kit

---

## 1. Brand Essence

**Promise:**  
Professional social media videos, delivered consistently, so businesses can grow without stress.

**Purpose:**  
Give small businesses a modern, confident online presence through high-quality short-form video and a process so simple they actually use it.

**Personality:**  
Professional · Calm · Human · Practical · Reliable

**Values:**  
- Consistency over intensity  
- Clarity over cleverness  
- Craft over speed  
- Respect for people's time  

---

## 2. Messaging

**Primary Headline:**  
Social media that actually gets done.

**Supporting Lines:**  
- Professional short-form videos, delivered weekly.  
- We handle the ideas, scripts, editing, and captions — you post and grow.  
- Better content. Less stress. More customers.

**Proof Points:**  
- Ready in 3 minutes  
- No call required  
- Most videos delivered within 48–72 hours  
- Cancel anytime  

---

## 3. Tone of Voice

**Characteristics:**  
- Short sentences  
- Direct, not blunt  
- Calm, not cold  
- Friendly, not casual  
- Dutch straightforwardness  

**Rules:**  
- No jargon  
- No hype  
- No AI messaging  
- No exclamation marks in headlines  
- One idea per sentence  

**Examples:**

| Context | Write This | Not This |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Hero | Social media that actually gets done. | Supercharge your social presence! |
| Value prop | We handle the hard part. | Our comprehensive solution manages all aspects of content creation. |
| CTA | Get Started | Start Your Journey |
| Reassurance | No call required. | Skip the hassle of scheduling calls! |

---

## 4. Colour Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| BOM Black | #0C0C0C | Primary text, headers, buttons |
| Warm White | #FAF9F7 | Backgrounds |
| Paper White | #FFFFFF | Cards, inputs, contrast areas |
| Slate Grey | #2E2E2E | Secondary text |
| Steel Grey | #5A5A5A | Supporting text, captions |
| Silver Mist | #E8E6E3 | Borders, dividers |
| Stone Blue | #6B8EA3 | Links, interactive elements |
| Sage | #9BA88A | Success states, positive emphasis |

**Rules:**  
- No gradients  
- No neon  
- Stone Blue for links and clickable elements  
- Sage for success, confirmation, and subtle emphasis  
- When in doubt, use black and white  

---

## 5. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Headlines | Michroma | All caps acceptable for hero only |
| Body | Inter | 16–18px, line-height 1.5 |

**Rules:**  
- Max line width: 70 characters  
- No more than two weights per page (Regular, Medium or Semi-Bold)  
- Body text always left-aligned  

---

## 6. Logo

**Primary:**  
- Black on white (default)  
- White on black (dark sections)

**Monogram:**  
The bomb-shaped "O" — use for favicon, watermarks, small icons.

**Clear Space:**  
Minimum clear space = height of the "O"

**Minimum Sizes:**  
- Full logo: 120px width  
- Monogram: 20px width  

---

## 7. Photography & Video

**Use imagery that feels:**  
- Real, not staged  
- Local (Dutch/European)  
- Slightly desaturated  
- Natural lighting  
- Clean composition  

**Avoid:**  
- Neon or cyberpunk aesthetics  
- American corporate stock  
- Overly stylised anything  
- Busy backgrounds  

---

## 8. Iconography

- Thin outline style  
- Geometric, minimal  
- 1.5px stroke weight  
- Stone Blue for interactive, Sage for success states  

---

## 9. Internal Principles

We keep things simple.  
We communicate clearly.  
We deliver consistently.  
We don't overpromise.  
We don't hype trends.  
We focus on relief, not stress.  
We show up as real humans.
